Image Name Info
Siamese cat This breed is very friendly, and social. They really like human interaction and usually like to play with their owners.
Snowshoe cat Like the Siamese cat, the Snowshoe cat is very playful and also intelligent. This breed usually likes to jump on the owner's lap.
Shorthair cat This breed is very friendly, and extroverted, it is also known as one of the most friendly cats.
Scottish fold cat This breed is very fluffy and cute. They are very also sweet and easy to own.
Persian cat This breed is very playful and energetic at times, one moment they could be very lazy, the next they could be running around the house just like a little kitten.
Ragdoll cat This breed is also classified as one of the most friendly breeds, very loving to its owners.
Bengal cat While it may seem that this breed is a wild cat, it isn't. it is a domesticated and very sweet cat. they are also very curious.
Maine coon cat The marine coon breed is also a sweet and gentle cat and can adapt to most environments but their diet should be watched as if they aren't monitored, they will become very very fat.
Singapura cat This cat is very energetic and is very smart, they are also very curious. they don't really like loud noises and it scares them.
Japanese Bobtail cat The Japanese Bobtail is smart enough understand when their owner's call them by their name. they are also active and sweet.
